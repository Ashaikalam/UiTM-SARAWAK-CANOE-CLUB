Objectives

1. Promotion of Water Sports:

To actively promote and engage in a variety ofwater sports activities, including kayaking, rafting, rowing, dragon boat racing, and traditional Sarawak boat racing

2. Skills Development:

To facilitate skill development and proficiency in these water sports among club members.

3. Competition Participation:

To represent UiTM Sarawak and compete in regional and national water sports competitions, striving for success andrecognition.

4. Fostering a Healthy Lifestyle:

To encourage a healthy and active lifestyle within the university community through the enjoyment and participation inwater sports.
